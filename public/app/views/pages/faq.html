Frequently Asked Questions

As a business Owner, how can I apply to your platform?

You either click on join us in the navigation bar or on the apply icon in the bottom of the website, to fill in the form of the application.

How to know whether my application has been accepted or rejected?

After submitting your application, our team will check your application thoroughly then they will send you an email to inform you about the results of the investigations.

As a user, how can I cancel my reservation?

Once you login, you have the rights to see all your reservations and you can cancel them if you want, as long as you haven't exceeded the cancelation window of that reservation.

As a user, How can I find the venues that support my prefered activity type?

You will find the activities that our businesses offer in the home page, once you click on the activity you like, you would get all the businesses that support your preferences.
